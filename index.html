About me

Divyesh Unadkat is a Ph.D. Student in the Centre for Formal Design and Verification of Software (CFDVS) at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IITB). He is pursuing his Ph.D. in Software Verification under the supervision of Prof. Supratik Chakraborty and Prof. Ashutosh Gupta. He has received his Bachelor of Engineering degree in Computer Engineering from Dharmsinh Desai University (DDU). He is also currently affiliated as a Researcher with the Foundations of Computing Group, TCS Research, Tata Research Development & Design Center (TRDDC), Pune.
